Once upon a time, there was a country which was full of monster. In the highest tower of the country, there lived a king of all monsters called Mephisto. 
One day, Mephisto heard that the neighbour of his country, there was king who had a beautiful daugther, named Aqua. Mephisto wanted to see how beautiful she was. Therefore, he ordered his servant to bring the princess back to his country. The king was very angry, he ordered all the warriors in the country, who could bring back his daughter, would be the next king. Then, a warrior named Arthur heard it. He left his home and started on his journey to rescue the princess.
